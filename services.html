Processing

At UNICO Hub, we process secondary raw materials in strict adherence to our clients' specifications, which frequently necessitate material blending.

Blending and mixing are sometimes underestimated in their significance. However, they play a crucial role in transforming materials to meet the exact characteristics desired by our customers.

As a result, we process waste materials, customizing them to the specifications of refineries and smelters. Our robust analytical capabilities enable us to acquire materials with confidence, ensuring that our clients receive products precisely tailored to their requirements.

Trade & Delivery

We facilitate the trade and delivery of these materials for metal extraction to globally recognized recycling plants that are publicly listed. Our comprehensive resources, integrated systems, and streamlined processes guarantee the efficient operation and delivery of these materials.

"At UNICO, we specialize in the recycling of scrap metal and plastic. We work with a diverse range of products that contain various types of metals."
