UNICO Plastics is an industrial plastic recycling firm that purchases various types of scrap plastic, which include:

  1. Plastic pallets
  2. Collapsible (knockdown) bins
  3. Crates, totes, and buckets
  4. Plastic purge, lumps, and chunks
  5. Repro pallets and virgin off-spec resin.

Please note that we do not handle residential plastics. Also, be aware that the industrial plastics we purchase must be clean and separated before pick-up.

What We Do with It

Your scrap plastic holds value for us because we process it for resale. Our process is straightforward: we come to your location to collect the scrap plastic you have available. We then transport the plastic to one of our facilities, where it undergoes processing through a series of machines designed to reduce it to three-eighths of an inch regrind.

The regrind material is subsequently processed through a series of magnets to remove any metal debris. It is then packaged and prepared for sale. We supply plastic regrind to manufacturers for use in their end products.
