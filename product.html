EPR Compliance

EPR, or Extended Producer Responsibility, is a concept that places the responsibility for the disposal and management of a product's waste on the brand, manufacturer, and importers. In India, the Central Pollution Control Board has mandated EPR for plastic waste through the Plastic Waste Management Rules of 2016. This policy requires plastic producers to take actions to collect and recycle post-consumer plastic waste. Brand owners, producers, and importers (PIBOs) must establish collection systems or partner with authorized waste management agencies to fulfill their EPR obligations.

For Indian brands, producers, or importers that use plastic in their packaging, there is a responsibility to reduce environmental impact and demonstrate a commitment to sustainability. UNICO's Waste Management offers EPR Compliance Services to assist these entities in India in achieving EPR compliance.
